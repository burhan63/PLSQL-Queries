==how to update the serial of vehicles==
Step No: 01
sub sy pehle policy no lainge or uska assorted code uthaye gey or phr check karey gey 
kai wo fleed hai yah sheduled hai
Query:
select * from 
ins_assorted ia 
where ia.assorted_string like '%POLICY NO%'; 

Step No:02
phr hum us assorted code per check kare gey vehicles ki detail 
Query:
select * from mt_vehicles_details vd 
where vd.assorted_code = 'ASSORTED_CODE';

Step No:03 
or phr hum query mai for update laga kar vehicle seq no ko change kardey gey 
Query:
select * from mt_vehicles_details vd 
where vd.assorted_code = 'ASSORTED_CODE'
for update;
