pehli bar salaam takaful sy li iska matlab yeh fresh hua

RENEWAL: means kai apki policy hai or wo 1 saal yah kuch period bad expire hone wali hai

ROLLBACK: means agar meri aik policy pak qatar mai hai laikin mujhy packages samjh nh aye tw 
mai nai salaam takaful mai lai li iska matlab roll back hai
