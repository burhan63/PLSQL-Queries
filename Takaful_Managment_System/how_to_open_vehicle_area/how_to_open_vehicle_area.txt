== how to open vehicle area ==

Step No: 01: sub sy pehle endno/policyno/certificate no aye tw isey check karna hai
or assorted ode get karna hai. is query ki madad sy 

Query:
select * from ins_assorted ia 
where ia.assorted_string like '%endno/policyno/certificate no%';

Step No: 02: assorted code uthane kai kai bad mt_vehicle_details mai dal daina hai

Query: 
select * from mt_vehicle_details vd 
where vd,assorted_code = 'Assorted_Code'
for update;
